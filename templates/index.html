    An AI based Automated Attendance System

    In a technologically powerful world like ours, a viral infection like COVID19 had us all panicked and assigned to homes. Though the lockdown state-wide and country-wide is shutting out rapidly, there is a very good need to stay secure, hygienic and cautious of the overall surroundings at all times until the foreseeable future. Workplaces being the second home to any adult, it is time to upgrade both our habits and procedural systems.

    Fingerprint reader being the most widely used biometric system of the decade, it is also a point of contact between users and hence also a point of contact for viruses like SARS-CoV2. There arises a need to replace fingerprint based biometric authentication and attendance systems with face recognition based systems. Implementation of the same is now made easier keeping accuracy intact. Welcome to Face Digital, a face recognition based attendance system for the new world where we learn to survive alongside covid.
